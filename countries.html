Countries I Want to Visit

Japan

Cherry blossoms, futuristic cities, peaceful temples, and delicious sushi. A perfect blend of tradition and innovation.

Italy

Home of pizza, pasta, and art. From the canals of Venice to the ruins of Rome — every corner tells a story.

Canada

Majestic mountains, stunning lakes, and friendly people. Canada is nature’s masterpiece in every season.

Korea

Vibrant pop culture, high-tech cities, spicy food, and rich traditions. Korea is energy and elegance in one.

Paris

The city of love and lights. Eiffel Tower, croissants, art museums — romance lives in every Parisian street.

Spain

Flamenco, sunshine, and architecture that makes you stare. Spain is passion, color, and lively nights.

London

Historic palaces, afternoon tea, red buses, and modern vibes. London blends the royal and the urban effortlessly.
